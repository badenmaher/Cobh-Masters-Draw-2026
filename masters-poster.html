⛳ How It Works
  1. Each entry costs €10
  2. 100 Masters field players are split into 4 brackets (25 players each) by world ranking
  3. Each entrant picks 2 players from each bracket — 8 players total
  4. Your score = the combined score vs par of all 8 picks over 4 rounds
  5. Only scores from rounds your pick makes the cut count
  6. Leaderboard updates live online every 3 minutes
  7. Lowest combined score wins! (Under par = negative = good)
